About me

I'm an IT Engineer with a passion for frontend development. For the past three years, I've worked as a Technical Artist in the game industry, where I combined my technical skills with creativity to bridge the gap between artists and developers. This experience has sharpened my attention to detail and deepened my understanding of both the technical and aesthetic aspects of digital creation.

I've completed a frontend developer bootcamp, which allowed me to deepen my expertise in creating intuitive, responsive, and visually appealing user interfaces. I've also undertaken several frontend projects on my own, each one reinforcing my enthusiasm for building user-friendly web applications.

Now, I'm excited to take the next step in my career and apply my skills in a professional setting. I'm eager to contribute to a dynamic team, tackle new challenges, and continue growing as a frontend developer.
